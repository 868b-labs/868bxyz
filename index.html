About 868b Labs Inc.

From 2022 to 2025, I led the Ethereum Foundation’s Ecosystem Support Program, overseeing thousands of grant reviews and directing funding to over 350 projects across developer tooling, ZK research, security, and protocol development. This role provided a rare, ground-level view of what makes projects credible, resilient, and sustainable in the Ethereum ecosystem.

Before that, I spent two decades leading billion-dollar space robotics programs, designing and maintaining mission-critical systems where failure was not an option. That experience instilled a pragmatic, risk-aware, and outcomes-driven approach that continues to guide my work.

Through 868b Labs Inc., I now apply this combined experience to help organizations navigate complex decisions, design credible programs, and build sustainable systems that last.
